DESIGN BRIEF Why it looks like this
Positioning
The only Tunisian QR-ordering with a true native app + AI. Design must feel a full class above Scanny / Digital Menu TN — premium, appetizing, trustworthy for owners.
Direction — “Harissa & Sidi Bou”
Warm terracotta (appetite, craft) × deep Mediterranean teal (trust, Sidi Bou Saïd). Warm cream neutrals, an 8-point zellige star as the mark. No gradients, no clutter.
Type & language
Bricolage Grotesque (display) + Manrope (UI) + IBM Plex Sans Arabic. French default, full RTL mirroring, Arabic headings ~10% larger, Western numerals for prices.
Principles
Android-first at 412px · 48px touch targets · order-only, pay at counter (stated, never hidden) · designed offline states · dark mode where it earns it (kitchen) · demo venue: Café El Marsa, La Marsa.
